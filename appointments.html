Preparation Tips

  • Bring inspiration images or Pinterest boards to help our stylists understand your vision
  • Wear or bring undergarments similar to what you'd wear with your new pieces
  • Consider your lifestyle needs and any upcoming special events
  • Note any specific colors, fabrics, or styles you prefer or wish to avoid
