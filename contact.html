Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can view shipping options at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for full-price items in original condition with tags attached. Sale items are final sale.

Do you offer alterations?

Yes, we offer complimentary basic alterations on full-price purchases. Additional custom alterations are available for a fee.

How do I care for my Riyo Secrets pieces?

Each garment comes with specific care instructions. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for most pieces and storing them properly to maintain their quality.
